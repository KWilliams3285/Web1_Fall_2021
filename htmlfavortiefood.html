Favorite Food #1: Chicken Alfredo

What is this food?
Chicken Alfredo is a combination of several different ingredients the main ones being Chicken, Pasta, and Alfredo sauce. Many different pastas are used to make chicken alfredo but the most popular is fettuccine. Alfredo sauce can be made at home with the proper supplies or you can just use ready made sauce in the jar although it's not as tasty in my opinion.

Why I like this food

  1. I love Chicken.
  2. I love Pasta
  3. I love combinding the two, to make one.

What is this food?
Chicken Ceasar Salad is a salad made of simply chicken, salad, and ceasar dressing. Grilled or fried chicken go perfectly with this salad. With crutons and shredded parmasean cheese to top it off you have the perfect lunch.
