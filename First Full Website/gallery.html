Favorite Pets

Siberian Husky

The husky is a medium-sized dog that is full of energy. They are best known for their incredible snow-pulling skills. They are sweet and friendly but also loyal and protective.

Corn Snake

The corn snake is the most common type of pet for beginner snake handlers. They come in lots of different colors, are dociles and rarely bite.

Rabbits

Rabbits are cute furry animals with long ears and short tails. There are many different breeds and colors. They are perfect companions and are sweet and cuddly.

Blue Pit

Pit bulls are seen as violet dogs but they can be really sweet and loyal animals. If you treat them right and show them love you have yourself the most protective and loving pet.
